Ruby for Good 2021

Our Event

The Ruby for Good DC conference is an annual event based out of the DC metro area where engineers, designers, product managers, and other do-gooders from all over the globe get together over a long weekend to work on or build projects that help our communities. Think of it as hanging out in a communal space for a weekend with a bunch of developers and designers to work on something useful. Participants stay on-site; hacking and socializing takes place in communal areas. Join us just for the fun of giving back! Questions? Check out the FAQ or drop us a note on Slack or via email at info@rubyforgood.org.

This year we are going to be holding the event at a gorgeous retreat space in the woods a little more than an hour outside of DC. With the pandemic and the stress of the past year, we wanted to get away from the hustle and bustle of the cities where we could de-stress with all of our friends both new and old. Safety Information

For the safety of all, we are requiring that all attendees be fully vaccinated (2 weeks after date of last shot) in order to attend this event. Proof must be submitted, at the latest, 2 week prior to the event with non-compliance resulting in ejection from the event.
